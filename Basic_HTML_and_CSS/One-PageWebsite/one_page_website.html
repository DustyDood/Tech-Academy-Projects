Video Game Music

Hopefully you're open to video game music because there is some great stuff out there.

In fact, we're even going to give you three options!

Chrono Trigger

A renowned hit, Chrono Trigger's music was composed by Yasunori Mitsuda for the SNES, a 16 bit machine.

Nevertheless, the music truly evokes a sense of nostalgia and calm, regardless of the hardware's original limitation.

The World Ends With You

The World Ends With You was a game that flew under the radar for most folks, but it's a hit that shouldn't be missed.

The soundtrack has a bit more variety, having songs that provoke calm to accelerating anxiety. This intentional diversity represents Shibuya's diversity of cultures and ideas, a key theme and location for the game.

NieR Automata

A more modern video game, NieR automata's soundtrack is heavily inspired by classical music.

The overall theme of the music is somber, but it is something that should be listened to at least once.
